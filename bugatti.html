BUGATTI BOLIDE

The Bugatti Bolide is an extreme, lightweight hypercar that showcases the pinnacle of Bugatti engineering. Designed for maximum performance on the track, the Bolide boasts unparalleled aerodynamics and cutting-edge technology.

The Bugatti Bolide, being a limited production hypercar, has a price tag that reflects its exclusivity and advanced engineering. While the exact price can vary, it is estimated to be around:

  • Base Price: Approximately $4 million USD

Key Features

Some standout features of the Bugatti Bolide include:

  • 1,850 Horsepower W16 Engine: Unmatched power and performance.
  • Extreme Lightweight Construction: Advanced materials for maximum speed.
  • Innovative Aerodynamics: Design elements that enhance downforce and stability.
  • Track-Ready Configuration: Optimized for the racetrack, with top-tier handling and braking systems.
  • Limited Production: Exclusivity guaranteed, with only a few units available worldwide.
