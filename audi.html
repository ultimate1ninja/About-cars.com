Price

The price of an Audi A8 can vary based on the model year, trim level, and additional features. As of 2024, here are some general price ranges for the new Audi A8:

  • Base Model: Starting around $86,000
  • Premium Plus: Around $91,000 to $93,000
  • Prestige: Around $97,000 to $99,000
  • Executive: Around $100,000 to $105,000
  • Security Model: Over $120,000

These prices are for new models and can vary by region and dealership. Additionally, certified pre-owned Audi A8s and older models can be found at lower prices.
