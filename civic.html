Price

The price of a Honda Civic X can vary based on the model year, trim level, and additional features. Here are some general price ranges:

  • Base Model (LX trim): Starting around $22,000
  • Sport Trim: Around $23,000 to $24,000
  • EX Trim: Around $25,000 to $26,000
  • EX-L Trim: Around $27,000 to $28,000
  • Touring Trim: Around $29,000 to $30,000

These prices are for new models and can vary by region and dealership. Additionally, certified pre-owned Civics and older models can be found at lower prices.
